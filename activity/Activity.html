Describe & Draw

The fastest way to feel why clear communication matters. One of you describes a hidden picture; the other draws it — without ever seeing it.

Role A The Describer

You see the picture. Explain it in words only — shapes, sizes, positions. You cannot draw or gesture.

Role B The Drawer

You can't see the picture. Listen closely, ask good questions, and draw what you understand on the pad.
