The three pillars

01 · Active Listening

  • Full attention — phone down, face them.
  • Paraphrase: "So you're saying…"
  • Ask open questions; don't assume.
  • Let silence do some work.

02 · Written & Digital

  • Lead with the ask in line one.
  • Be specific — "Fri 3pm," not "soon."
  • Check the tone — read it aloud.
  • Over 3 messages? Just talk.

03 · Feedback

  • Give it with the S·B·I formula →
  • Receiving: breathe, get curious.
  • Ask for an example.
  • It's about the work, not you.

The feedback formula & the listening check

S · B · I — feedback that lands
S
Situation

When & where it happened.

B
Behaviour

What they actually did — facts, not labels.

I
Impact

The effect it had on you / the team.

"In today's meeting (S), you took notes for everyone (B), which kept us all on the same page — thank you! (I)"
